The Demo Files

A number of demonstration files are provided in the subdirectory demo. They show simple and typical ways to use the process library.

Because of the òprocessó aspect, and because of the nature of inter-process communication, the demo files come in small groups. For some groups, one program will manipulate others. For other groups, one may start a òserveró and some òclientsó and then interact with the clients to initiate communication. In the following, the groups are presented one by one.

pipeline, consumer and producer
firstProgram and otherProgram
streamcounterserver and streamcounterclient
binarycounterserver and binarycounterclient
xpilotgames
repChatClient and repChatServer
