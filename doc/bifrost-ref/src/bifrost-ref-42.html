PieShape

A Pie composed of a
PieShape and a Paint:

The PieSlice shape has the following attributes:

Center.
A Point specifying the center of the PieSlice shape.
HorizontalRadius and VerticalRadius.
The width and height of the PieSlice shape.
Angle1 and Angle2.
The two angles (in degrees).

A corresponding PieSlice graphical object is defined with PieShape as the shape. PieSlice uses FillPie of the Paint to draw itself (see Predefined Paint Operations).
