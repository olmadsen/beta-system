regexp_replace

Like regexp_search, except that it takes a second enter parameter, replacement_string. Regexp_replace searches for the regexp, and replaces the matched substring of THIS(text) with the replacement string. The replacement string may contain \0, \1, ..., \9, representing the substring matched by the iõth parenthesis in the regexp. \0 represents the entire substring matched. INNER is executed after the replace has taken place.
