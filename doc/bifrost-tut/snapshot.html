Appendix B. A Screen Snapshot

The screen snapshot below shows an execution of the final program from Appendix A. It shows a situation where several Stations have been added, and Rails have been added to connect various of them. The Palette has been made visible by typing 'P', and in the Palette, the "Move-Mode" has been selected. In the main window, the Station marked 'D' has been clicked on with the mouse and the Station is being dragged. Notice how the Rails connecting it with other Stations stick to the Station D during the dragging.
