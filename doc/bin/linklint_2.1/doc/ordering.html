Linklint is Shareware

Linklint started out as freeware. I was looking for a fast, high quality, low cost link checker that would work on Windows and Unix. I couldn't find one so I decided to write my own. As more people used Linklint and gave me feedback, maintenance and improvement of the program turned into a full time occupation.

I am trying to provide the internet community with a high quality tool for checking web sites. I can continue with this effort only if people who use the program help support the cost of its development.
