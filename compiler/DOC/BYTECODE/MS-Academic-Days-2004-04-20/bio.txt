
Peter Andersen (http://www.daimi.au.dk/~datpete)is an academic
researcher at the Computer Science Department of the University of
Aarhus, Denmark (http://www.daimi.au.dk).  Peter is affiliated with
the research group on object-oriented languages, and has been
researching and implementing OO languages, compilers, run-time
systems, garbage-collectors etc. since his M.Sc. in 1992 from the
University of Aarhus.

As a co-owner and periodically employee of the IT-consulting company
Mjølner Informatics A/S, Peter has had the role of technical lead in
industry projects ranging from instruction-set simulators and JITters
to computer graphics.
